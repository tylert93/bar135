WE HAVE SOMETHING FOR EVERYONE

We offer a refreshing twist on the modern cocktail bar. With a great range of signature and classic cocktails, complemented by our hand-picked selection of popular beers and wines, we have something for everyone. On draught, we carry ale, larger and cider to accommodate for all tastes. We also offer soft drinks, juices, iced tea and non-alcoholic beer for those wishing to take it easy.

OPEN UNTIL 3AM WEEKENDS, 2AM MIDWEEK

At Bar 135, were always here for those who don’t wish to head home early. Thanks to our underground location, were able to keep the party going late into the early hours when the surrounding bars and pubs have closed their doors. So if it’s a lively night you’re looking for, why not pop in and say hello.

PERFECT FOR INTAMATE PARTIES

With a capacity of 75 people, our cosy and stylish bar is the perfect setting for those special gatherings with friends, family and colleagues. It's large enough to accommodate for all your needs and small and enough to create a packed and intimate atmosphere. You can also give the bar your own personal touch. You're free to come in ahead of time and set up with your choice of decorations, pictures and nibbles. You can even set up your own lighting rig and DJ booth!. If you wish you can play your personalised playlist using our Spotify or plug your own device into our sound system.

FREE EVENING HIRE

Our bar is available for exclusive hire, either until 11pm or all night, absolutely free of charge so long as the minimum spend is reached. So, the more you spend the more you save. The bar is also available for hire during the day.

BRING THE BAR TO YOU

We're not confined to the four walls and have a wealth of experience running professional pop up bars. We can provide mobile bars for all types of occasions - from private parties at home to mass-scale events to high profile weddings.

COMMITTED TO CONSERVATION AND SUSTAINABILITY

COCKTAILS FOR CONSERVATION

With a background in both conservation and hospitality we thought, why not put these two ideas together – and here we are! Apparently were not the only ones who think the two go hand in hand and we’ve teamed up with various brands that support Wildlife Conservation. In order to promote these products we’ve developed a ‘cocktails for conservation’ menu, with £1 from every drink sold going towards conservation charities – at present these are the David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust and Marine Conservation Society.

ETHICALLY CONSCIOUS

We’ve also teamed up with the likes of FAIR, ChariTea and Lemonaid+, who collectively support Fairtrade, make their products from organic ingredients and contribute to social projects in the growing regions of their ingredients.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY

One of our main focuses at the bar is to be as sustainable as we can, from using biodegradable straws and bin liners, composting all our fruit slices and recycling waste materials. Even our menus are made from elephant dung!

COCKTAIL MASTERCLASSES

Our Masterclasses are a great way for you and your friends your hand at the art of mixology. They take a leisurely but informative approach to cocktails, with an emphasis being on tasting what makes a great drink. They are perfect for dates and social functions alike. The masterclasses can be played in teams, pitting you against your friends.
