EVENING HIRE


Fee: Deposit of £200 refunded once minimum spend is hit.


Minimum Spend: Minimum spend of £800 if you would like to hire it until 11pm and £1,200 for the whole night. If you hire it exclusively until 11pm then when the doors are open all public spend afterwards goes towards your minimum spend.


DAY-TIME HIRE


Fee: £75/hour

TERMS


  • CAPACITY : 75
  • COST : Refundable deposit to book the venue, minimum spend on the night.
  • DECORATION etc : You can bring your own balloons, decorations, etc. Unfortunately we do not allow confetti.
  • DRINK DEALS : We offer discounts on pre-ordered drinks should you wish to have a specific allocation or drinks on arrival.
  • DRINKS ON ARRIVAL : If you would like to provide your own then there's a small corkage which will go towards your min spend.
  • MUSIC : You can either provide a playlist or provide your own DJ, providing a DJ will reduce the capacity to 65. Live music is allowed in the venue.
  • FOOD : We offer canapes, sharing boards, buffet, courtesy of Papadeli. Or you can bring your own cold snacks.
