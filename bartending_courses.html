BARTENDING COURSES

ENHANCE YOUR CV - DEVELOP YOUR SKILLS - GAIN CONFIDENCE

Our Academy will help develop your skills in the dynamic and burgeoning field of cocktail bartending. The knowledge, techniques and experience you'll gain will make you more employable within the catering industry. You could also benefit from immediate work opportunities with us at events and in our permanent bar. Our Academy will help build confidence in yourself by understanding what it takes to provide good customer service and a good product.

Have a look below at the different courses we provide and decide which would be best suitable to your needs

* Discounts are available for multiple bookings *


4 DAY FULL COURSE - £450


  • A detailed look at the different types of spirit and distillation techniques, including tastings.
  • The role of the bartender in modern day society, including the customer journey and the sale.
  • The techniques required of the bartender, understanding the equipment, glassware, shaking techniques, free pouring, etc.
  • The formula of cocktails and different styles.
  • Cocktail recipes.
  • Examinations on the final day.
